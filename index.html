Frequently asked questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We have no MOQs for fulfillment if you have existing daily volume. For custom branding and private labeling, MOQs typically start as low as 50 units depending on the material.

How fast is shipping to the US and Europe?

Our private line shipping averages 5-8 days to the US and 6-9 days to major European countries. We provide end-to-end tracking for all parcels.

Do you offer payment terms?

Yes, we offer credit lines and flexible payment terms for partners with consistent order volume and a proven track record.

How do I connect my store?

We connect directly with Shopify, WooCommerce, and other major platforms via our app. Orders are automatically pulled into our system for immediate processing.

I already have a supplier. is it difficult to switch to you?

It's seamless. We coordinate the transfer of existing stock or simply start with your new inventory. Our onboarding team handles the heavy lifting so you don't experience any downtime.
